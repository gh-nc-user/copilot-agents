## 💡 Tips for Success

### Tip 1: Start with Quick Reference
Don't jump straight to the detailed guide. Read PHASE_5_QUICK_REFERENCE.md first (5 min) to understand the flow.

### Tip 2: Set Aside 30 Minutes
Phase 5 requires UI interactions that can't be automated. Block out 30 minutes where you won't be interrupted.

### Tip 3: Test Early
As soon as you publish the first agent, test it in Teams. Don't wait to publish all 4.

### Tip 4: Customize the Runbook
Before sharing TEAMS_RUNBOOK.md with end-users, customize it with:
- Your company name/logo
- Your support contact info
- Your internal processes
- Links to your resources

### Tip 5: Keep Instructions Handy
Open PHASE_5_PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md in one window and Copilot Studio in another.
