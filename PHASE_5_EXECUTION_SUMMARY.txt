Problem: Agents don't appear in Teams after 10 minutes
Solution: 
1. Wait 5 more minutes (sync delay)
2. Refresh Teams (Ctrl+Shift+R)
3. Verify agents are "Published" (not "Draft")
4. Check you're in correct Teams organization
5. See: PHASE_5_PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md (Troubleshooting)

Problem: Agent times out during testing
Solution:
1. Try with simpler deal query
2. Check internet connection stability
3. Verify specialist agents are published
4. Check Power Platform capacity
5. Retry the test
6. See: PHASE_5_PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md (Troubleshooting)

Problem: Memo format is wrong or incomplete
Solution:
1. Verify Deal Memo Writer Agent has all 5 sections
2. Check confidence field mapping
3. Run test again (may be transient)
4. See: PHASE_5_PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md (Troubleshooting)

Problem: Can't find Copilot in Teams
Solution:
1. Check Teams sidebar for Copilot icon
2. Search for "Copilot" in Teams search
3. Verify Teams is updated to latest version
4. Restart Teams client
5. See: PHASE_5_PUBLISHING_GUIDE.md (Troubleshooting)
